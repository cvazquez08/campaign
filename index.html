Meet Chris Koval

When the question is asked “Who is Looking Out for Us?”, the answer will be “Chris Koval, because Koval Cares”. Chris Koval – the most recent candidate for the Miramar City Commission Seat 1 – has been a Miramar resident for the past 14 years, and has resided in the greater Broward County area for 52 of his 54 years of life. He is also the business owner and Publisher for Inside Miramar/Pembroke Pines Magazine; a publication that highlights current events, local businesses and restaurants. Chris loves South Florida and knows the entire Miramar landscape like the back of his hand, as he is constantly meeting with business owners, from Historic Miramar to West Miramar, and everywhere in between. His passion is in connecting with the local people and getting involved with his community; it is both how and where he thrives. His wife, Nancy Koval, has been an American Citizen for over 15 years and was born in Peru. Nancy is a successful business woman, and together, her and Chris have 2 beautiful daughters.
