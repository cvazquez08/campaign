Meet Chris Koval

When the question is asked “Who is Looking Out for Us?”, the answer will be “Chris Koval, because Koval Cares”. Chris is a 14 year Miramar resident and owns a small business in Miramar, where he publishes a magazine. His goal has always been to help small businesses promote their products and services to the local residents. He is a proud Broward County resident for over 50 years. Nancy, Chris’ wife, is originally from Peru, and became a proud American citizen over 10 years ago. She is a successful business woman in her own right. Together, Chris and her have been raising their two beautiful daughters since 2006; they were both born at Memorial Hospital Miramar. His roots reach many places. His late step-mother was from Jamaica. Chris is proud to have a background that is reflective of the City of Miramar.

Koval Cares Priorities

Long gone will be the days when the community is left in the dark about issues that affect their quality of life. Chris Koval won’t just be running to be your next City Commissioner, but he will be the one that watches out for the residents of Miramar once elected. Currently, there has been little consideration to how the decisions made by the elected have been impacting the quality of life of Miramar residents both now, and in the future. Chris can be the change; never leaving you out of the bigger picture. Why? Because Koval Cares.
  • Prior to taking action will seek community input, no more big surprises in OUR neighborhoods.
  • Cares about our community and maintaining and improving quality of life for all residents.
  • Inconveniencing residents is no longer an option. City projects that get started in Historic Miramar will get finished in a timely manner.
  • Will enhance 21st Century Policing policies. "A safe city is a prosperous city".
  • Is a Miramar business owner experienced and qualified to eliminate waste and prioritize spending in our city budget.
